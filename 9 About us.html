About Us

Kudos, synonym of honor. It’s one of the fastest growing sectors in Bangladesh is the fast-food and restaurant sectors. Initially, it was growing in the capital city of Dhaka. Nowadays, it has gradually spread all over the divisional cities and district towns. One of them is Kudos, which started its journey from a food truck and later established the fast-food restaurant idea. Now it is one of the most hyped brands in Bangladesh’s fast food and restaurant sectors. The founders of Kudos are three friends: Rezaur Rahman, Torikul Hossain Polash, and Mehrab Lodi. They became a good friends when they studied together at the same university. They started to sell fast food like shawarma, burgers, and fries in trucks in the Dhaka University area. In a short period, Kudos gained popularity among its customers. Since they had no idea of running a food truck business in our country, they were facing legal issues. As a result, they started looking for a permanent restaurant to solve this problem. And they opened their first restaurant branch in Dhaka. Nowadays, Kudos have ten franchises, of which they sell BDT 1 million each. And now they have a 10 thousand square foot factory where they prepare raw materials for food for these franchises.
