Frequently Asked Question

Kudos does deliver to my area but my address is not found in the search results. What should I do?


We are constantly updating our mapping system to include all the addresses covered within each restaurant's devery radius. Please write to us via our Customer's Review section .
In the meantime, you may call 1300-9999 for further assistance


How much is the minimum order requirement for devery service?


Our current minimum devery order is RM18 excluding devery charges and goods and services GST.


Is Kudos' devery service available 24-hours a day, 7 days a week?


Kudos' devery service is available 24-hours a day, 7 days a week at selected areas. Please refer to our devery coverage st on this site for more information.


Can I make changes after I've made my order or can I cancel my order?


Yes, you may make changes or cancel the order by caling 1300-99-6969, provided that the items have not left the restaurant. Please check on the "Track Order" tab to check on the status of your orders.
